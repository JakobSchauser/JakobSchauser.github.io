With focus on the gastrulation, we have explored morphogenesis through the lens of Drosophila Melanogaster.

Taking our onset in embryogenesis, we seek to understand how cells interact and communicate during development.

Watch the Magic Happen!

A visualization of Drosophila morphogenesis.

As can be seen above, we have a full-embryo simulation of the first 12 minutes of Drosophila gastrulation, where the cells are shown to be moving in a coordinated manner. Our model rely purely on local interactions between cells, with no global information relaying or time-dependance.
